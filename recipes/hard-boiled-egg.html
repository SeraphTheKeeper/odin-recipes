Hard Boiled Eggs

Home

These are going to be the best hard-boiled eggs you've ever made.

Today, we're going to focus on making the simplest, perfect hard-boiled eggs that you can enjoy with many staple dishes. We can include them in a hot bowl of ramen, chopped up in a cobb salad, or simply enjoying them with some salt and mayo. You'll soon discover that boiled-eggs can quickly steal your heart.

Ingredients

For our ingredients, we only need a handful of items:

  • A medium-sized pot
  • 2 large eggs
  • Cold water

Steps

  1. First, place them in the medium-sized pot and and cover the eggs with cold water by 1 inch (2.54cm).
  2. Bring the water to a boil over high heat.
  3. As soon as the water begins to boil, turn off the heat.
  4. Cover the pot and let the eggs sit for 10-12 minutes.
  5. Transfer the eggs into a cold bath and leave them there for another 14 minutes before you peel them.
  6. Take the eggs one by one and rap them against a plate or counter and peel the shell away little by little.
  7. Smacznego!
