Schnitzel

A mouthwatering plate of schnitzel that you'll never get tired of eating.

On some days, you have some extra time and you want to pull out all the stops to make something truly heartwarming. This schnitzel recipe will be so good, you'll be willing to put in the extra time to make it again and again.

Ingredients

  • 2lb (32oz) pork chops
  • 1/2cups (64g) all-purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2cups (256g) breading
  • 2cups (16fl oz) vegetable oil
  • 1tbs (15g) Vegeta (optional)
  • Freshly ground pepper
  • Large sautee pan

Steps

  1. First off, I like to work out some of my daily stress by laying out the pork chops on some saran wrap, covering the top with more saran, and tenderizing the pork with a kitchen mallet.
  2. Next, we preheat our pan to medium heat. Pour in enough oil to cover the bottom of the pan about a 1/4 inch (0.6cm) deep.
  3. While our pan is preheating, whisk our eggs and seasoning in a bowl. In a separate bowl pour out our flour, and in another bowl pour out the breading.
  4. Now that our station is prepared and oil is heated, take each pork chop and pat it down on all sides with flour, then eggs, then breading.
  5. Drop each chop into the pan, making sure you drop them away from you so the hot oil doesn't splash you. Each side should fry for about 5-8 minutes until the breading is golden brown and the pork has turned white.
  6. Transfer your schnitzel to a wire rack or place on paper towels to drain excess oil. Serve with lemon, parsley, mashed potatoes, and vegetable of choice.
  7. Smacznego!
